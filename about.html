About

What "The Good Lens" means — the lens of the mind, not the camera; seeing grace even in what looks like ruin; becoming innocent like a child; the flower that still gives its fragrance even when crushed.

Who's writing this — a little about you, and about your Guru and the tradition or teaching you're drawing from, as much or as little as you want to share.

What to expect here — photo-essays, not a portfolio; gratitude, grace, and prayer as recurring threads; written slowly, posted whenever something moves you to.
